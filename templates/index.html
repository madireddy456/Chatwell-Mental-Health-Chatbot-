Description

"ChatWell" is a mental health chatbot designed to provide support, guidance, and resources to individuals seeking assistance with their mental well-being. This AI-driven chatbot offers a safe and confidential space for users to express their thoughts, feelings, and concerns related to mental health.

Through natural language understanding (NLU) technology, ChatWell can engage in meaningful conversations with users, empathetically responding to their queries and providing personalized support based on their unique needs.
